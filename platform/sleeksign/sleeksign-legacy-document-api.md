## Evidence

### sleek-sign-backend

- `routes/document.js` — URL constants and handler wiring for the endpoints listed in the **Short Description** block.
- `app.js` — `app.use("/document", require("./routes/document"))` (no `tenantIdGateway` on this mount; tenant handling may be inside handlers — confirm per environment). Related mounts: `/user-signatures`, `/initials`, `/user`, `/wfe-upload`, `/uploads`, `/platform-companies`, `/webhooks`, `/document-trail`, `/tenant-ids`.

### customer-mfe

- `customer-mfe/sleek-sign/src/proxies/back-end/sleeksign/*.js` — e.g. `sign-document.js`, `document-list.js`, `finalize-draft-document.js`, `void-document.js`, `get-document-signing-url.js`, etc.
